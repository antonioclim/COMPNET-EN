Lab Guide

Stage 3: App-Layer Traffic

Unlike standard L2/L3 routing, SDN allows policies based on application protocols.

  • TCP to h2: Allowed.
  • TCP to h3: Blocked by controller.
  • UDP to h3: Initial policy is DROP. Use the controller panel to update the flow table to ALLOW.

Notice how TCP requires a handshake (blocked immediately), while UDP sends data regardless of the receiver's state.

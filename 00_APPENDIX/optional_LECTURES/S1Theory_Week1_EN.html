üî¨ delay of transmission vs. delay of propagation

üü¢ delay of transmission (dtrans)

Timpul for a impinge toti bitsi packageului on link.

dtrans = L / R

L = size package (bits), R = width of bandwidth (bits/sec)

üîµ delay of propagation (dprop)

Timpul for ca un bit to calatoreasca de-a lungul linkului.

dprop = d / s

d = away (m), s = ~2√ó10‚Å∏ m/s in fibre

üí° Observatie Critica

A link of 1 Gbps transmite of 10√ó more repede decat 100 Mbps, but delaya of propagation remains unchanged ‚Äî limited by the speed of light. intercontinental latency cannot drop below ~50ms.
